We Are In A Mission To Help The Helpless

Career Development

This section of Samvedana is, in fact, the sole reason of Samvedana's existence. Lots of talented students from rural India struggles when they come to cities and compete with the urban world. Lack of global exposure leads to weak communication and presentation. Lack of confidence kills the risk taking abilities, and in results, it limits the growth! This section of Samvedana is trying to empower students with soft skills and counselling from respective domain experts. A network of alumni students is building a bridge between rural and urban India, and helping youngsters to find right opportunities where they can compete and succeed. Career counselling through workshops, training programs and guest lectures are few of the key activities being done under this section. In long run, Samvedana aspire to work on creating the employment opportunities on local level itself. May it be via partnership with local entrepreneurs or by helping and assisting aspiring entrepreneurs to set up their businesses, Samvedana would do everything possible to improve the rural economy as well as lifestyle of people living in Ajara.
